## LINKEDIN ACTIVITY SYSTEM

### The Post Rhythm
One post per week minimum. Sunday. Non-negotiable.
Content already defined in Concern 7 and the challenge framework.

### The Connection Strategy
10+ new connections per week starting now.
Not random. Targeted.

**Who to connect with:**
- DevOps engineers in DFW specifically (use location filter)
- People who commented on posts from the DevOps content you already follow
- Junior DevOps engineers who recently got hired (they're active about it)
- Recruiters at companies in DFW with DevOps teams

**How to find them:**
Search "DevOps engineer" + filter location to Dallas-Fort Worth.
Click on a post from someone like Loweh Goodness or similar.
Click into the comments. Every commenter is a potential connection.
That's the commenter rabbit hole method.

**Connection request note (always include one):**
"Hey [name] — building toward a Junior DevOps role in DFW.
Following your content, learning a lot. Would love to connect."
Short. Direct. No fluff.

### The Commenting Strategy
2 posts per day. Monday through Friday.
Comment on DevOps people's content. Not "great post!" — that's noise.

**Comment formula:** Acknowledge + Add + Ask
- Acknowledge: "This is exactly right about [specific thing they said]"
- Add: "I ran into this when [specific thing you did]"
- Ask: "How do you [specific question]?"

Minimum 15 words. Shorter gets no algorithmic weight.
Comments in the first 60 minutes after a post goes live get the most visibility.

**Who to comment on:**
Tier 1 — comment on every post, every time:
Loweh Goodness, Danny Thompson, TechWorld with Nana, Forrest Brazeal,
Leon Noel, the person from the LinkedIn screenshot you shared

Tier 2 — comment when relevant:
Anyone in DFW DevOps space
Anyone posting about tools you're currently learning

### The 117 Impressions Signal
One post reached 117 impressions and 69 members with 1 connection.
That's proof the content works. The distribution didn't.
With 50 targeted connections the same post reaches 500+.
With 200 connections it reaches thousands.
The content is already working. Build the distribution.
